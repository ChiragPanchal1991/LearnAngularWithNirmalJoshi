Slice pipe with text
Full text:- {{text}}
  • Display first 10 charactors:- {{text|slice:0:10}}
  • Display last 10 charactors:- {{text|slice:-10}}
  • Remove last 10 charactors:- {{text|slice:0:-10}}
Slice pipe with Array
Full array:- {{array}}
  • Display first 2 item from array:- {{array|slice:0:2}}
  • Display last 2 item from array:- {{array|slice:-2}}
  • Remove last 3 item from arrays:- {{array|slice:0:-3}}
